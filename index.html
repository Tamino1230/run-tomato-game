About the Game

Get ready for a juicy adventure! In RUN-TOMATO, you are a brave tomato on a mission to escape the clutches of hungry orcs. Fight back, and run for your life in this action-packed 2D game!

The game is a fast-paced survival game where you have to be quick on your feet (or... stem?).
